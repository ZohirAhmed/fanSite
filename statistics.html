Statistics in vainglory are based off of two major ratios K/D/A and your win/lose ratio. The first ratio is known as KDA or kill, death, assist ratio. The way this ratio is solved is by adding the amount of kills and assists you have first and then you divide that number by the amount of deaths you have. The win/lose ratio is simply putting the totals amount of win you have divided by your total amount of games. The final important statistic is your rank, this displays your overall skill in vainglory because the higher your rank the more wins you have in matches that really matter. These are some of the multiple vainglory statistics.
